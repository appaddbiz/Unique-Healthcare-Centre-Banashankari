Dr. Pavankumar P Rasalkar

MD (AIIMS), DM (PGIMER), FSCAI (USA)

Senior Consultant Interventional Cardiology

Dr. Pavankumar P Rasalkar is a highly accomplished Senior Consultant Interventional Cardiologist with training from AIIMS (New Delhi), PGIMER (Chandigarh), and FSCAI (USA). He has served in leading hospitals and academic institutions, including as Associate Professor & HOD of Cardiology at KIMS, Bangalore, and as Senior Consultant at Fortis, Apollo, and Sagar Hospitals. Currently, he practices at Manipal Hospital, Kanakapura Road and is the Founder Director & Lead Consultant at Unique Healthcare Centre, Banashankari Bengaluru. With extensive expertise in complex interventions, intracoronary imaging, device therapies, and preventive cardiology, he is recognized as a leading cardiologist in Bangalore.

Career Objective To achieve excellence in the field of Interventional Cardiology, with a strong academic orientation and research interest.
