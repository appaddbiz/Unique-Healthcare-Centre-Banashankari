All types of Blood Tests

All types of blood tests detect infections, monitor organ function, assess overall health, hormones, immunity, and diagnose various conditions.

Ultrasound Scans

Ultrasound scans use high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images of internal organs, blood flow, pregnancy, and soft tissue structures.

ECG

ECG (Electrocardiogram) is a quick, non-invasive test that records the electrical activity of the heart to detect abnormalities and monitor cardiac health.

ECHO

ECHO (Echocardiogram) uses ultrasound to create images of the heart, helping assess structure and function for accurate cardiac diagnosis.

TMT

TMT (Treadmill Test) evaluates the heart's response to physical stress and helps detect blockages or reduced blood flow to the heart.

2D Scan

2D Scans provide real-time black-and-white imaging to monitor organs, detect abnormalities, and guide diagnosis in various medical conditions.

Holter Test

The Holter Test continuously records your heart’s rhythm over 24 to 48 hours, helping detect irregular heartbeats that may not appear during an ECG.
