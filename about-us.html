Welcome to Unique Healthcare Centre

Unique Healthcare Centre is a leading multispeciality clinic located in the heart of Banashankari, dedicated to offering comprehensive healthcare services. We specialize in consultations with highly skilled doctors in Cardiology and Diabetology, ensuring top-notch care for our patients.

At Unique Healthcare Centre, we provide a wide range of services, including Laboratory & Imaging, OPD Consultation, Daycare & Minor OT, Pharmacy, and Physiotherapy. Our in-house services also cover ECG, ECHO, Lipid Profile, Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS), HbA1c tests, and a fully equipped blood collection laboratory, all managed by qualified professionals who prioritize your health and comfort.

Our team of doctors is internationally trained and highly experienced, with decades of expertise in treating various cardio and diabetes-related conditions. They are deeply committed to patient care and work together to ensure that all patients receive personalized and effective treatment.

At Unique Healthcare Centre, we focus on delivering quality, ethical, and cost-effective healthcare to all our patients. We strive to provide excellent care while maintaining minimal charges to make healthcare accessible to everyone.

If you are looking for the best multispecialty clinic near you, look no further. Our approachable and compassionate doctors, combined with our state-of-the-art facilities, make us the top choice in Banashankari for all your healthcare needs.

24/7 Care & Support

Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

Our on-site laboratory and imaging facilities provide quick, reliable test results to help guide your treatment plan.

Our **Cardiology** and **Diabetology** departments offer specialized care for heart disease, diabetes, and related conditions. We create personalized treatment plans for each patient to ensure the best outcomes.

We use state-of-the-art diagnostic tools, including **ECG**, **ECHO**, and **Blood Tests**, to provide accurate results for effective diagnosis and treatment.

We offer a full spectrum of healthcare services, from **OPD Consultation** and **Daycare Services** to **Physiotherapy** and **Pharmacy**, ensuring seamless care from start to finish.

Health emergencies can happen at any time. That's why we offer **24/7 emergency care** with a team of qualified professionals ready to respond to your needs. Whether it’s a sudden cardiac event or urgent diabetic care, our specialists are available around the clock to provide immediate assistance and help stabilize your condition.

At Unique Healthcare Centre, we believe in offering high-quality care at affordable prices. We are committed to providing clear, transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Whether you need a routine consultation, specialized treatment, or diagnostic tests, you can trust that we offer **cost-effective healthcare services** that focus on both quality and value.

Skip the hassle of searching for pharmacies outside. Our **in-house pharmacy** provides all prescribed medications conveniently at the clinic. With a range of essential medicines available, we ensure that you get the right medication immediately after your consultation, making your healthcare journey more convenient.
