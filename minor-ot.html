Minor OT (Operating Theatre) at Unique Healthcare Centre

At Unique Healthcare Centre, our Minor OT (Operating Theatre) provides a safe, sterile, and efficient environment for patients requiring minor surgical procedures. Our team of skilled surgeons and medical staff are dedicated to delivering high-quality care with the utmost attention to patient safety and comfort.

Whether it’s a routine procedure, a diagnostic operation, or treatment of a minor injury, our Minor OT is equipped with advanced technology and staffed by experienced professionals to ensure the best possible outcomes. We prioritize your well-being and recovery at every step of the process.

The Minor OT services we provide include:

  • Wound care and dressing changes
  • Abscess drainage
  • Minor cosmetic and dermatologic surgeries
  • Endoscopic procedures (e.g., cystoscopy, colonoscopy)
  • Biopsy and minor tissue removal
  • Orthopedic procedures (fracture fixation, joint aspirations)
  • Minor dental surgeries
  • IV line insertion and minor injections
  • Removal of foreign objects from the body

Our Minor OT is designed to handle less invasive procedures that do not require extended recovery times or overnight stays. We ensure a smooth and efficient experience, allowing patients to return to their daily activities as soon as possible, with the necessary follow-up care provided for optimal recovery.

Why Choose Unique Healthcare Centre for Minor OT Services?

  • Experienced surgeons and medical staff with specialized expertise
  • State-of-the-art equipment and facilities
  • Safe, sterile environment to minimize risk
  • Minimally invasive techniques to reduce recovery time
  • Comprehensive post-operative care and support

Your health and safety are our top priority. At Unique Healthcare Centre, we ensure that your minor surgical needs are met with the highest standard of care and precision.
