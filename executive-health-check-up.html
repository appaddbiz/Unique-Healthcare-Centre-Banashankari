What is Executive Health check-up?

Executive health checks are just another type of modern day health check-ups made for giving people an idea about their overall health. So, it is vital to take time to prevent major medical concerns. An executive check-up is also an assessment which involves different medical procedures aimed at preventing health issues.

We give the best health check-up in Banashankari. Visit us today.

Unique Healthcare Centre offers quality services at huge discounts in the places of assessment and medical care research.

Why Executive Health check-up is important?

The aim of Executive Health Check Up is to extend the healthy life expectancy of executives, to avoid untimely death, and to improve the quality of life for executives with a disease.

Advantages of Executive Health Check-up?

  • It helps in avoiding shocks about personnel’s health.
  • It helps to plan and formulate the right strategies in advance, both medical and work-related upon knowing of underlying health issues.
  • It helps every employee gauge the state of their health and take requisite steps likely to result in higher productivity, lower downtime, and absenteeism.
  • In the case of contagious diseases, timely health checks may reduce the likelihood of the spread of diseases.

What set of people require this?

Packages can even be designed depending upon the age-group of executives and the ratio of men versus women.
