OPD Consultation Facility at Unique Healthcare Centre

At Unique Healthcare Centre, our OPD (Outpatient Department) Consultation facility serves as the first point of contact for patients seeking medical advice, diagnosis, and treatment. We provide a comfortable and patient-friendly environment where you can consult with experienced specialists across various medical disciplines.

Our OPD services are designed to offer prompt, efficient, and affordable medical consultations without the need for hospital admission. Whether you have a minor health concern or a chronic condition that needs regular monitoring, our outpatient services ensure that you receive quality care and timely follow-ups.

Key features of our OPD consultation facility include:

  • Consultation with general physicians and specialist doctors
  • Preliminary diagnosis and health assessment
  • Prescription and medication advice
  • Routine health check-ups and preventive care
  • Follow-up consultations for ongoing treatments
  • On-site support services like lab testing, pharmacy, and imaging

Our OPD operates on a flexible schedule, with appointments and walk-ins available for patient convenience. Each consultation is focused on understanding your concerns, providing clear guidance, and recommending appropriate next steps—be it further testing, treatment, or lifestyle changes.

Why Choose Unique Healthcare Centre for OPD Consultations?

  • Experienced and patient-friendly doctors
  • Multi-specialty consultations under one roof
  • Minimal wait times with appointment-based services
  • Integrated support services like pharmacy, lab, and imaging
  • Clean, modern, and accessible consultation facilities

At Unique Healthcare Centre, your well-being is our priority. Our OPD services are focused on timely, expert care with compassion and professionalism—ensuring a seamless healthcare experience for you and your family.

Book your OPD consultation today and get trusted medical advice from our expert team.
